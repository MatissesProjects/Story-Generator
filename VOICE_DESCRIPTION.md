American English Voices
The Narrator / Strong Male: en_US-ryan-high

Vibe: Deep, resonant, and highly professional. It was trained specifically on audiobook data, making it arguably the best open-source male voice for storytelling.

The Female Lead: en_US-lessac-high

Vibe: You already found the medium version of this! It’s clear, articulate, and very expressive. Upgrading to the high version will give you slightly better intonation for emotional scenes.

The Friendly/Younger Male: en_US-joe-medium

Vibe: A bit higher pitched than Ryan, sounds more conversational, upbeat, and modern. Excellent for a sidekick or a younger protagonist.

The Younger Female: en_US-amy-medium

Vibe: Brighter and slightly faster-paced. Great for a highly energetic or younger character to contrast with the more measured Lessac voice.

🇬🇧 British English Voices (For the "English Leaning" variety)
The Mature British Male: en_GB-alan-medium

Vibe: Distinctly British, older, and authoritative. Perfect for an older mentor, a villain, or a distinguished supporting character.

The Polished British Female: en_GB-jenny_dioco-medium

Vibe: Very clean, standard British pronunciation. Sounds intelligent and steady.

The Scottish/Regional Female: en_GB-alba-medium

Vibe: Has a distinct Scottish lilt! If you want a character to immediately sound like they are from a different region than the rest of your cast, this is a fantastic choice.
