# 🤖 Track: Proactive NPC Agency

## Overview
Transform NPCs from reactive participants into goal-oriented agents with internal motivations and autonomous behaviors.

## Core Objectives
- **Utility AI**: Implement a need-based decision system (Hunger, Social, Ambition).
- **Goal Pursuit**: Allow NPCs to travel between locations to fulfill their goals.
- **Autonomous Interaction**: NPCs can form relationships with each other, not just the player.
