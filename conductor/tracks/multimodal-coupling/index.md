# Track: Multimodal Coupling (Atmospheric Sync)

## Objective
Tightly couple visual and audio cues with the narrative state, ensuring that the atmosphere directly reflects the current tension and emotional arc.

## Key Goals
- [ ] Link **Ambient Lighting (TINT)** to the Narrative Tension Curve.
- [ ] Synchronize **Music Intensity** with the Drama Manager's state.
- [ ] Trigger **Leitmotifs** and specific sound effects based on emotional appraisals.
